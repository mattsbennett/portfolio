Heads or Tails
by Matthew Bennett
Project Description
A celebration of brand identity in the automotive world. Each vehicle presented boasts a legacy, either derived from raw performance and racing heritage or from a combination of economy and performance that appealed to the masses. Besides these qualities, which contributed significantly to their success as consumer products, each of these vehicles has a distinctive visual aesthetic that has undoubtedly influenced the design direction of their greater brand. Heads or Tails is a study and celebration of the changing faces of these automotive icons using minimalist illustration to juxtapose old and new.
